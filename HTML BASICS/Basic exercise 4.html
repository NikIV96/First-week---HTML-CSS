Top Gun 2: Maverick

Plot Summary

Top Gun: Maverick is the long-awaited sequel to the 1986 hit movie Top Gun. In this sequel, Maverick (Tom Cruise) is a test pilot who still longs to fly fighter jets, but is now facing an uncertain future. He is sent back to Top Gun as a flight instructor, where he must confront his past and mentor a new generation of pilots.

Cast

Tom Cruise as Pete "Maverick" Mitchell

Cruise reprises his role as Maverick, a highly skilled pilot who is now facing the end of his career.

Miles Teller as Bradley "Rooster" Bradshaw

Teller plays the son of Maverick's former flying partner, who is now a pilot in his own right.

Jennifer Connelly as Penny Benjamin

Connelly plays a single mother who runs a bar near the Navy base.

Release Date

Top Gun: Maverick was originally scheduled to be released in July 2019, but the release date was pushed back to June 26, 2020. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the release date was delayed again and finally released in November 19, 2021.
